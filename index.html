The History Of NJENAC COLLECTIONS

NJENAC COLLECTIONS was started in the year 2014 by our founder Nancy Njeri after hitting rock bottom. She started with a bale of Ksh.1000. This bale had fancy tops and chiffon tops.

She was later joined by our investors, who helped the business grow. We are now found countrywide in our branches, having our main branch in Nairobi.

NJENAC COLLECTIONS has been participating in various activities like uplifting women by encouraging them to be the better versions of themselves. We have employed more than one hundred of these women, supporting them in the education of their children.
